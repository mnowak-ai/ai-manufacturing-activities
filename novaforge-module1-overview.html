Mission: Inside the NovaForge Smart Factory

Your first operational mission starts here. Read your briefing before Phase A begins.

01   Welcome

Welcome to NovaForge Robotics

You've been assigned to the operations team at NovaForge Robotics — one of the most advanced manufacturing facilities in the country. This is a smart factory, meaning AI systems are running alongside human workers every single day.

Before you can take on real responsibilities here, you need to understand how this factory works — where AI fits in, where human judgment is still essential, and how the two work together to keep production moving safely.

This is your first mission inside the plant. Pay attention. The team is counting on you.

02   Mission Briefing

What's Happening at NovaForge

NovaForge is ramping up production of its newest product: the NovaBot — a warehouse robot used by logistics companies across the country. That means the factory is under pressure to move faster, work smarter, and stay safe.

Leadership has made a decision: AI systems will be used across all major operations. But here's the challenge — not every operations team member understands how these systems work, what decisions they make, and when a human needs to step in.

That's your mission. You'll investigate the factory floor, learn the systems, and develop the judgment to work alongside AI effectively.

The Challenge

Production is expanding. AI tools are in place — but the team needs to understand how to use them safely and effectively.

The Stakes

Misjudging an AI alert or skipping a human review could cause safety issues, defects, or production delays.

Your Assignment

Investigate AI use across the factory. Learn where it helps, where it falls short, and where humans must lead.

The Outcome

By the end of this mission, you'll be ready to make smart, confident operational decisions in an AI-powered workplace.

03   Your Objectives

What You'll Do in This Mission

This isn't a lecture. You're going into the factory. Here's what you'll be doing across Phase A, Phase B, and Phase C:

  • 01 Explore how NovaForge's operations work from the ground up
  • 02 Identify where AI tools are being used — and what they're doing
  • 03 Evaluate workplace safety risks and how AI helps detect them
  • 04 Determine which decisions belong to AI systems and which require a human call
  • 05 Analyze how AI is changing the pace and structure of manufacturing workflows
  • 06 Practice making real operational decisions using the information you gather
04   Inside the Smart Factory

The Areas You'll Investigate

NovaForge is a large facility. You won't be wandering — you have specific areas to visit. Each one runs differently, and each one uses AI in a different way.

🦾 Zone 1
Robotics Assembly Line

Where NovaBot robots are built, piece by piece. Machines move fast here. AI monitors every step — and flags anything that looks off before it becomes a real problem.

🔬 Zone 2
Quality Control Lab

Every unit gets inspected before it leaves. AI-powered cameras scan for defects too small for the human eye — but a human tech makes the final call on whether to pass or reject a unit.

🔧 Zone 3
Maintenance Department

The team here keeps machines running. AI tells them which machines are starting to wear down — sometimes days before a breakdown would happen. Less downtime means more production.

📦 Zone 4
Warehouse Automation Area

Finished NovaBot units are sorted, tracked, and staged for shipping. Automated inventory systems do the heavy lifting — but human coordinators manage exceptions and scheduling.

🖥️ Zone 5
Operations Command Center

This is the brain of the factory. Real-time dashboards show what's happening across every zone — production rates, equipment status, safety alerts, and staffing. Leadership makes decisions here using AI-generated data, but human judgment still drives every major call.
