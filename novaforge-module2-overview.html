01   Welcome to the Investigation

Something Is Wrong at NovaForge

NovaForge Robotics is one of the most advanced manufacturing facilities in the country — but right now, its flagship production line is in trouble. The NovaBot assembly line is experiencing repeated disruptions, and no one on the leadership team has a clear answer yet.

Shipment deadlines are approaching. Equipment errors are piling up. Downtime is increasing. And leadership needs the operations team to find the root cause before the situation gets worse.

That's where you come in. You've been brought onto the NovaForge Operations Investigation Team — a group of specialists assigned to dig into the data, review the evidence, and help the factory get back on track.

This is not a routine shift. This is an active investigation. And it starts now.

NovaForge operations teams use AI tools as part of their diagnostic and troubleshooting workflow. In this mission, you'll learn how — and when — to use them effectively.

Diagnostic Support

Critical reminder: AI tools help your team analyze data faster — but they do not understand the factory floor the way experienced workers do. AI can miss context, misread conditions, and generate incomplete recommendations. In this mission, every AI output must be reviewed by a human before any action is taken. That human is you.
